# Hagai Heshes — hagaiheshes.com

## Product Purpose
A minimal, sharp personal consulting website for Hagai Heshes — "The Crystallizer." Primarily a trust-validation page for referred visitors, not a lead-generation machine. One scroll-based page. No subpages.

## Users
Senior decision-makers at B2B tech companies (Israel and US market) who were referred to Hagai. They arrive already warm — this page must confirm, not convince.

## Brand & Tone
- Quiet confidence. No fluff. White space does the work.
- Sharp, editorial, precise — like a senior independent consultant's page.
- Positioning: "I distill what you're really about — then help you take it to market."
- NOT a design thinker or innovation consultant. Specifically: GTM strategy, positioning, go-to-market for companies that can't afford to be misunderstood.

## Anti-references
- No SaaS landing page patterns (hero metrics, gradient accents, identical card grids)
- No consultant buzzword soup
- No decorative icons or illustrations beyond the crystal
- No gradients on backgrounds
- No shadows (except extremely subtle)

## Design Principles
- Flat, minimal, white background
- Typography and horizontal rules carry all structure
- One visual element: the crystal (hero, right side)
- Generous white space between sections
- Mobile responsive, fast load

## Color System
- --ink: #0f0f0e (near-black, warm tint)
- --bg: #fafaf9 (warm off-white)
- --gold: #b8975a (accent, used sparingly)
- Strategy: Restrained — tinted neutrals + one gold accent

## Typography
- Display/headings: Playfair Display (serif)
- Body/UI: Plus Jakarta Sans
- Scale: 10.5px labels → 15–16px body → 26–30px section titles → 48–76px hero

## Sections (in order)
1. Nav — sticky, thin border, name left + links right
2. Hero — split 50/50, text left + crystal right
3. Crystallization + GTM — two pillars
4. Clients — grid + logo marquee
5. How I Work
6. What You Walk Away With
7. About — photo (logos/hagai.jpeg) + bio text
8. Media / Press — YouTube embeds
9. Contact — dark background
10. Footer
